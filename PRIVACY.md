# Privacy Policy for Wikidata Reconciler for Google Sheets

Last Updated: 5 March 2026

1. **Introduction** This Privacy Policy applies to the "Wikidata Reconciler" Google Sheets Add-on ("the Add-on"). By using the Add-on, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.
1. **Data We Access and Use** The Add-on requires access to your Google Sheets to function correctly. Specifically, it accesses:
- Active Cell Data: The Add-on reads the text in the cells you actively select to use as a search query.
- Spreadsheet Edits: The Add-on writes the data retrieved from Wikidata (such as QIDs, labels, and descriptions) directly back into your spreadsheet.
1. **Third-Party Services (Wikidata)** Search terms are sent to the Wikidata API (wikidata.org) to retrieve matching entities. Please note that data transmitted to Wikidata is subject to Wikimedia's privacy policies. We recommend avoiding the use of this tool on cells containing sensitive or confidential personal information, as we do not control how external APIs process search queries. No data is shared with any other third parties.
1. **Data Storage and Retention** We do not collect, store, log, or share your personal information or spreadsheet data on any external servers. All data processing occurs between your Google account and the Wikidata public API. The developer of this Add-on has no access to your spreadsheets or your search history.
1. **Google API Services User Data Policy** The Add-on's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
1. **Contact** If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact: wikidata-reconciler@okf.fi
