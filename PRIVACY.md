# Privacy Policy for Wikidata Reconciler for Google Sheets

1. Data Collection: Wikidata Reconciler for Google Sheets does not collect or store any personal user data.
1. Data Usage: The app only accesses the active Spreadsheet to retrieve search terms provided by the user and to write Wikidata results back to that specific spreadsheet.
1. Third-Party Sharing: Search terms are sent to the Wikidata API (wikidata.org) to retrieve matching entities. No data is shared with any other third parties.
1. Data Retention: No data is retained by the developer. All processing happens within the user's Google Workspace environment and the Wikidata public API.
1. Contact: For questions, contact dev@okf.fi.
